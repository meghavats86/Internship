WHAT IS BMI?

Body Mass Index (BMI) is a measure of body fat based on height and weight. It helps assess health risks associated with weight, aiding in understanding one's overall health status

What does BMI indicate?

BMI (Body Mass Index) indicates a person's body fatness based on their weight and height. It categorizes individuals into underweight, normal weight, overweight, or obese ranges, providing a general assessment of health risks related to weight.
